Self-balancing Lego Car

#Assembly #DE2 #NIOS2 #ElectricalMotor #LightSensor


How it works:
  • Assemble the axles, gears, plate together into see-saw car
  • Connect motor and sensor port to lego controller, then link to GPIO of DE2 Board
  • light sensor value indicate closeness to the ground
  • If left side is closer to ground, set motor direction to forward and rise, vice versa
  • Use Pulse Width Modulation to adjust motor speed
